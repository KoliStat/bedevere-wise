Bedevere Wise — /embed harness

Drives an iframe to /embed with a configurable URL, posts embed-theme / embed-run at it, and logs embed-resize / embed-ready back from the iframe. Open this page from the Vite dev server (http://localhost:3000/testfiles/embed-harness.html) so the iframe is same-origin with the served fixtures under http://localhost:3000/testfiles/*.
